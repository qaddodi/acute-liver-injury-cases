ACUTE LIVER INJURY — BACKGROUND IMAGE ART DIRECTION
Interactive Case Deck · backgrounds for the self-contained HTML

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO USE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Generate one 16:9 image per entry below. File names must match exactly
  (e.g. "ALI 06 Ischemic Hepatitis.png") so the deck picks them up.
• Each entry is a complete, copy-paste prompt: paste the SCENE line, then
  append the MASTER STYLE block, then append the NEGATIVE PROMPT.
• The deck overlays text with a soft light wash, so every image needs a
  calm, low-contrast "quiet zone" where the text sits. The COMPOSITION note
  tells you which side to keep quiet for that specific slide.
• Target 1920×1080 or larger. Consistent palette and lighting across all 21
  images is what makes the deck look professionally produced — treat the
  master style as fixed and vary only the scene.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
MASTER STYLE  (append to every prompt)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Clinical editorial illustration in the spirit of a New England Journal of
Medicine cover: delicate watercolor washes over fine architectural ink
linework, restrained and intelligent, never cartoonish. Warm ivory paper
texture with a faint tooth. Cohesive muted palette — slate blue-gray
(#5d6e8c), soft ochre (#ae8845), dusty rose (#a45d65), sage (#61724c) — low
saturation, gentle atmospheric depth, a single soft light source. Anatomy and
lab equipment are accurate but understated. Generous negative space, elegant
composition, print-quality detail, 16:9 widescreen. Full-bleed: the artwork and
its paper texture fill the entire frame edge to edge, with the wash blending
seamlessly into the margins — no borders, no framed panel, no inset rectangle,
no hard vertical seam between a colored block and a blank area. Colors are soft
but present and confident, not pale, bleached, or washed out; the deck dims each
image slightly, so render it a touch richer than a pastel.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
NEGATIVE PROMPT  (append to every prompt)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
no text, no letters, no numbers, no labels, no captions, no words, no logos,
no watermark, no UI, no charts with readable values, no signatures, no
oversaturated colors, no neon, no harsh contrast, no clutter, no gore, no
distorted anatomy, no stock-photo faces, no 3D-render look, no borders, no
frame, no inset panel, no rectangular block, no hard vertical seam, no vignette
bars, not washed out, not bleached, not overly pale.
